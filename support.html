Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use this app?

Once installed, go to your Google Drive. Right-click on any Google Document (Google Docs only), hover over Open with, and select HTML Viewer from the list. The application will open in a new tab, request temporary permission to view that specific file, and then render the HTML.

Why is the app asking for permissions?

To convert your Google Document into HTML, the app needs permission to read the file. We use the safest, most restricted Google permission scope (drive.file), which means the app can only see the specific document you clicked on. It cannot see or access any other files in your Drive.

How do I uninstall the app?

If you no longer want to use the application, you can easily remove it:

  1. Go to your Google Drive.
  2. Click the Settings gear icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select Manage apps on the left-hand side.
  4. Scroll down until you find [Your App Name].
  5. Click the Options dropdown and select Disconnect from Drive.
